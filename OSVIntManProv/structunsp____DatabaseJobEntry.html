char* unsp__DatabaseJobEntry::Type

A bitmask that describes the job attributes.

The type may contain one or more attributes such as:

  • BASIC (no attributes)
  • CANCELABLE (job may be canceled)
  • SCHEDULABLE (job may be scheduled)
  • BLOCK_ON_DONE (job is blocked when completed)
  • BLOCK_ON_ERR (job is blocked when an error occurs)
  • TRAP_ON_ERR (trap is sent when job ends in error)
  • WRITES_LOG (log file is written upon completion of the job)


default value[range]: NA
