xsd__short* unsp__DeleteCACPolicyRequest::CACPolicyType

Indicates the CAC policy type to be deleted.

Policy type is a bitmap, where its bits represent the FaxPolicy, VoicePolicy, and VideoPolicy:

  • 0x0004 (VideoPolicyType)
  • 0x0005 (Voice & VideoPolicyType)
  • 0x0006 (FaxPolicyType)
  • 0x0007 (all three of the above)


required: true
