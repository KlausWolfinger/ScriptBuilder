Detailed Description

Business Group Department Definitions and Methods.

Business Groups can further be divided into departments and Business Group Lines can then be assigned to them. There are two variants of BG Departmens:

  • Billing
  • Emergency Calling

The department name can be delivered as an alternative to the calling or connected party name.
