Detailed Description

Business Group Subnet Definitions and Methods.

Business group subnet administration provides the BG administrator with the ability to provision the E9-1-1 Subnet Table on a per-BG basis. The purpose of a BG subnet is to associate one or more LINs (Location Identification Numbers) with a particular physical location to be used by an emergency operator, also known as a PSAP (Public Safety Answering Point). The physical location is expressed with either an IP address/ Subnet mask and/or Location Domain name.
