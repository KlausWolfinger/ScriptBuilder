Detailed Description

Broadcast Group Definitions and Methods.

Broadcast Groups are used to configure 1-way Speaker Broadcast capabilities for subscribers. Subscribers that are members to a Broadcast Group have the ability to initiate and/or receive Broadcast Calls depending on the setup. Members whose Role is 'Initiator' are able to broadcast over the speakers of the 'Receivers' in a Broadcast Group. Broadcast members with the role of 'Receiver' and which have 'Do not Disturb' enabled will not be receiving Broadcast Calls. An OSV subscriber can be a member of many Broadcast Groups concurrently and its role may vary in the different groups. The maximum number of members per Broadcast Group is 50 and a Broadcast Group in order to be operational must have at least two members: an 'Initiator' and a 'Receiver'. The maximum number of Broadcast Groups per system is 500.
