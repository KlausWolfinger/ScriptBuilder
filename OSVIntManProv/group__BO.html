Detailed Description

Branch Office Definitions and Methods.

A branch office is an entity of related subscribers and endpoints and is a sub entity of a business group (BG). Subscribers and endpoints belonging to a BG may optionally belong to a branch office. It is necessary to provide the name of the representative endpoint of the branch office when creating or updating a subscriber or endpoint in order to assign the subscriber/endpoint to the branch office. The maximum number of branch offices supported on the system is 3000.

Moreover, this section includes the methods to assign multiple Media Servers to a Branch Office.
