The number of times that the intercept treatment sequence is applied.

xsd__string InterceptData::DefaultName

Identifies a default intercept entry.

If a DefaultName is assigned, then all of its corresponding assigned treatments also become the treatments used, unless they are explicitly modified to be different.
required: false
