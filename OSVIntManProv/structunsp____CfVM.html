Detailed Description

Call Forwarding Voice Mail.

This service indicates that the subscriber has Voice Mail. VoiceMail is a combination of CfCFDA and CfCFB. These services are mutually exclusive and an attempt to add VM or either CFDA or CFB when the other is provisioned will result in an error.

MP2 command: CFVM [Attributes]
