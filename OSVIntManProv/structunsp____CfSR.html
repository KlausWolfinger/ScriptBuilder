Detailed Description

Station Restrictions.

Station Restrictions is a Business Group (BG) feature consisting of the following sub-features. It is possible to assign a BG line with both originating and terminating types of restrictions. Regardless of restrictions, lines within a BG can make calls to and receive calls from other lines within the BG, although forwardings, transfers, and attendant access may be restricted. When a call is restricted, reorder tone is provided. There is no intercept provisionable for this.

MP2 command: CFSR [Attributes]
