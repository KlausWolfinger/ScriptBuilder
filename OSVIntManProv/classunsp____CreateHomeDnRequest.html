xsd__string unsp__CreateHomeDnRequest::EndCode

Indicates the last directory number in range.

If provided, it must meet the same conditions as StartCode. The digit string must be greater than or equal to that of StartCode. The length of the Office Code and the Home Dn can be up to 15 digits.
